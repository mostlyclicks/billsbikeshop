About Bill's Bike Shop

Bill's Bike Shop has been a cornerstone of the local cycling community for over two decades. What started as a small passion project has grown into a fully-fledged bike shop that serves riders of all ages and abilities across the region.

Our founder, Bill Henderson, opened the shop with a simple mission: to provide cyclists with high-quality bikes, expert service, and genuine support from people who truly understand and love cycling. Today, that same commitment guides everything we do.

Our Story

Bill's journey in cycling began in his teenage years, when a simple bike ride sparked a lifelong passion. Over the years, he became an expert mechanic and enthusiast, working with bikes both professionally and as a hobby. When the opportunity came to open his own shop, he jumped at the chance.

From humble beginnings in a small storefront, we've expanded our facilities and inventory to better serve our growing customer base. We've invested in modern repair equipment, expanded our product selection, and built a team of passionate cycling professionals who share Bill's vision.

Our Values

Quality

We believe in quality over quantity. Every bike and component we stock is carefully selected to meet our high standards.

Community

We're not just a shop—we're part of the local cycling community. We sponsor local events and support riders of all levels.

Expertise

Our team brings decades of combined experience. We're here to educate and empower cyclists to make informed decisions.
